Hi,today I am going to talk about...DOGS AND CATS!!!

Dogs

Dogs are domesticated carnivores belonging to the family of Canidae. They belong to the mammal category as female dogs can give birth to puppies. They also have mammary glands, and nourish the puppies with milk. Dogs are known to be good swimmers and also friendly and helpful to humans.

Cat

Cats are beloved domestic animals, known for their independence and playful nature. They are skilled hunters, often keeping homes free from rodents. Their soft fur, expressive eyes, and unique vocalizations like purring contribute to their charm. Cats enjoy both independent activities and cuddling with their human companions.

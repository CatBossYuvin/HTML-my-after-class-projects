    • How a steam engine works?

    A steam engine converts heat energy into mechanical energy using steam pressure. A boiler heats water to create high-pressure steam, which is then directed into a cylinder. The steam pressure pushes a piston back and forth, and this reciprocating motion is converted into rotational motion to power machinery
